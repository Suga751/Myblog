Mental Health Matter!!

MindMe is a Mental Health Mentorship web applicaton designed to provide a supportive outlet for youth between ages (13-18) that are experiencing any stressful circustances (depression, anxiety, issues at home or school etc.) and need to learn conflict resolution and adequate coping skills. Youth will be able to use the application to discuss their issues in a blog format, recieve 1:1 mentorship via telecomunication or instant messenger and connect with mental health resources in their communities. The mentors range from mental health professionals, parents, and volunteers who have a passion for youth. All mentors will receive training (brick and morter and virtual training available) from a Masters level mental health professional.
